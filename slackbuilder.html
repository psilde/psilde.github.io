Slackbuilder

AI Slack message composer

A native macOS desktop app for composing Slack messages with Claude AI. Write what you want to say in plain terms, then use AI to refine the tone, adjust the length, or make it more professional, all before it leaves the app. Designed to keep your hands on the keyboard and your focus on the message, not the interface.

Features
  • AI-assisted drafting powered by Claude: write rough, send polished
  • Tone and length controls: casual, professional, concise, or detailed
  • Direct Slack integration, send without switching apps
  • Native macOS performance via Tauri, lightweight, no Electron overhead
  • Keyboard-first workflow, compose and send entirely without the mouse
