Spotify artwork extractor

A simple utility for getting full-resolution artwork out of Spotify. Paste any track, album, or playlist URL and download the original image file, not the compressed thumbnail that ends up in screenshots or metadata tags. Useful for music producers, designers, or anyone who actually cares about image quality.

Features
  • Supports any Spotify URL: tracks, albums, playlists, and podcasts
  • Downloads original resolution artwork, not the compressed stream thumbnail
  • Batch export for albums and playlists
  • No login required, works with public Spotify content
